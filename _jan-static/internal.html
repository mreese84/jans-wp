Cooking School

Article Title

The Classes

Jan’s on 4th Cooking School is located in the North Valley of Albuquerque. Learn a variety of cooking recipes, skills, and techniques that will both appeal to the novice and challenge the most experienced chef. Group and private classes are available for you and your friends or family. Girls Night Out or Team Building has never been so fun!

The Kitchen

Our kitchen resides in a 100 year old adobe home that has been restored to include the highest quality commerical kitchen products. We have everything you need to prepare your food and enjoy the meal you have prepared. You simply cannot match the experience of cooking in this kitchen!

The Approach

Jan has a passion for food and educating new students about the art of cooking. From the basic to the complexed, learn how to be confident in the kitchen. Learn how to shop for the right type of produce, pair it with protein, and create healthy and fantastic meals. We keep the classes small so there is plenty of hands-on teaching and you won’t feel overwhelmed. We start the classes with a review of the recipes, then break into groups to prepare the food, once the food is prepared we sit and enjoy! You get to take a copy of the recipes you make in class, keep a binder and create a personal cookbook!

The Schedule

Visit our calendar to see when classes are being held. Classes have a maximum capacity of 12 people and must have at least 6 people for it to commence. Please reserve your space at least 24 hours in advance – to make sure we have enough groceries!
If you have an idea for a cooking class, submit your idea! We love to hear feedback on what YOU want to learn.
